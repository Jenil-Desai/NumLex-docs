The NumLex community is a diverse group of developers, mathematicians, and enthusiasts who contribute to the development and improvement of the package. We encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing.

## Join the Discussion

- **GitHub Discussions**: Join our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Jenil-Desai/NumLex/discussions) to ask questions, share ideas, and connect with other users.

- **Issues and Pull Requests**: Contribute to the project by opening issues or submitting pull requests. We value your input and feedback.

- **Social Media**: Follow us on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/desaijenil) for the latest updates and community news.

## Contributing

Want to contribute? Check out our [How to Contribute](../guides/how-to-contribute) guide for everything you need to know about getting involved.

## Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering an open and welcoming community. Please read our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/Jenil-Desai/NumLex/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to understand our expectations for participants.
