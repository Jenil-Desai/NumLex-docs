We welcome your ideas for new features and improvements to NumLex! If you have a feature request, please follow these guidelines:

## How to Submit

1. **Create a Detailed Description**: Provide a clear and detailed description of the feature you would like to see. Include the problem it solves and how it can enhance the package.
2. **Submit a Request**: Open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Jenil-Desai/NumLex) with the tag `feature-request`. Be sure to include all relevant information.
3. **Engage with the Community**: Participate in discussions about the feature request. Other users may have additional insights or suggestions.

## Evaluation Process

Our team reviews all feature requests and prioritizes them based on factors such as impact, feasibility, and community interest. We’ll update you on the status of your request and notify you if it’s accepted for future development.

Thank you for contributing to the evolution of NumLex!
