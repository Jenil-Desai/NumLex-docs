## Planned Features

We have exciting plans for the future of NumLex! Here’s a glimpse of what’s coming next:

- **New Modules**: Expanding to include new mathematical and statistical functions.
- **Enhanced User Interface**: Improving ease of use and integration with other tools.
- **Performance Improvements**: Optimizing performance for large datasets and complex calculations.
- **Extended Documentation**: Adding more examples and comprehensive guides.

## Milestones

- **Q4 2024**: Release of Version 1.1 with additional conversion functions.
- **Q1 2025**: Major update with advanced features and user-requested enhancements.

Stay tuned for updates and follow our [Changelog](/guides/changelog) for the latest information on new releases!
