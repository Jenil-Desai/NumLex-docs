The **Statistics** module in NumLex offers a variety of functions for statistical analysis, including calculations for means, medians, modes, var, and standard deviation.

## Functions

- **`ungrp_mean(data)`**: Calculates the mean for ungrouped data.

  <Code code={`mean = ungrp_mean([10, 20, 30, 40, 50])`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`grp_mean(data, frequencies)`**: Calculates the mean for grouped data.

  <Code code={`mean = grp_mean([10, 20, 30], [2, 3, 5])`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`median(data)`**: Finds the median of a dataset.

  <Code code={`med = median([10, 20, 30, 40, 50])`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`ungrp_mode(data, frequencies)`**: Determines the mode for ungrouped data.

  <Code code={`mode = ungrp_mode([10, 20, 20, 30, 40],[2, 3, 5,9])`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`raw_mode(data)`**: Determines the raw mode of data.

  <Code code={`mode = raw_mode([10, 20, 20, 30, 40])`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`var(data)`**: Calculates the variance of data.

  <Code code={`variance = var([10, 20, 30, 40, 50])`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`std_dev(data)`**: Calculates the standard deviation of data.

  <Code code={`std_deviation = std_dev([10, 20, 30, 40, 50])`} lang="python"></Code>
