The **Geometry** module in NumLex provides essential geometric functions for calculating areas of common shapes.

## Functions

- **`area_triangle(base, height)`**: Calculates the area of a triangle.

  <Code code={`area = area_triangle(base=10, height=5)`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`area_circle(radius)`**: Calculates the area of a circle.

  <Code code={`area = area_circle(radius=7)`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`area_rectangle(length, width)`**: Calculates the area of a rectangle.

  <Code code={`area = area_rectangle(length=8, width=4)`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`area_square(side)`**: Calculates the area of a square.

  <Code code={`area = area_square(side=5)`} lang="python"></Code>
