The **Temperature Conversion** module in NumLex provides functions to convert temperatures between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin.

## Functions

### Celsius to Other Scales

- **`celsius_to_fahrenheit(celsius)`**: Converts a temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit.

  <Code code={`fahrenheit = celsius_to_fahrenheit(25)`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`celsius_to_kelvin(celsius)`**: Converts a temperature from Celsius to Kelvin.

  <Code code={`kelvin = celsius_to_kelvin(25)`} lang="python"></Code>

### Fahrenheit to Other Scales

- **`fahrenheit_to_celsius(fahrenheit)`**: Converts a temperature from Fahrenheit to Celsius.

  <Code code={`celsius = fahrenheit_to_celsius(77)`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`fahrenheit_to_kelvin(fahrenheit)`**: Converts a temperature from Fahrenheit to Kelvin.

  <Code code={`kelvin = fahrenheit_to_kelvin(77)`} lang="python"></Code>

### Kelvin to Other Scales

- **`kelvin_to_celsius(kelvin)`**: Converts a temperature from Kelvin to Celsius.

  <Code code={`celsius = kelvin_to_celsius(298.15)`} lang="python"></Code>

- **`kelvin_to_fahrenheit(kelvin)`**: Converts a temperature from Kelvin to Fahrenheit.

  <Code code={`fahrenheit = kelvin_to_fahrenheit(298.15)`} lang="python"></Code>
